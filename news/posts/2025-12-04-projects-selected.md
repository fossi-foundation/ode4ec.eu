# Three ODE4EC projects selected for funding

At [EFECS 2025](https://efecs.eu/) it was announced that three project proposals under the ODE4EC
umbrella have been [selected](https://cms.efecs.eu/uploads/Session_7_2_Anton_Chichkov_b8cbcd3595.pdf) for funding. The three projects target the three
*streams* of the [HORIZON-JU-Chips-2025-IA-EDA](https://www.chips-ju.europa.eu/CallDetails/?id=7b809f65-2021-f011-9989-000d3ab33b4d) call:

 * **ODE4EC-AMS** will target open source EDA tools for analog and mixed signal designs
 * **ODE4EC-DIG** will target open source EDA tools for digital designs
 * **ODE4EC-PIV** will target open source tools for productivity of chip design, integration into flows and verification of chip designs

The goal is to come to a grant agreement and start mid 2026.
