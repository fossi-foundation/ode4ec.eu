# HiPEAC 2026

At [HiPEAC 2026](https://www.hipeac.net/2026/krakow/#/) the initiative organized a workshop [*Open Design Environment for European Chips*](https://www.hipeac.net/2026/krakow/#/program/8265/). While the three first projects have not yet started, the goal was to bring together the community and discuss challenges and opportunities of open source chip design tools.

[Find a report on the event here](/news/2026-01-28-hipeac).
